Recipe Guide for Ducks

Learn to cook from a magistical collection of ancestral ducky recipes. Enhance your meals by adding the magical flavour that many our duck ancestor used to make their food much healthier and richer!

Corn with a Ducky Magic

  1. Gather 100 gm of corn seeds from the farmer's garden
  2. Boil them until they are soft
  3. Place them on a Leaf plate
  4. Add a pinch of salt to it
  5. Add a pinch of pepper (Aunt OldDuck's magic ingredient!)
  6. Enjoy your meal!

Macedonia for Ducks

  1. Go to a nearby park to steal 10 grapes
  2. Slice them neatly into 4 pieces each
  3. Decorate them onto a Leaf plate
  4. Add a pinch of pond weed
  5. Enjoy your delicious breakfast!

Duck exclusive Fish Soup

  1. Hunt a small fresh salmon
  2. Add 15 gm of insects (of your choice!)
  3. Add a pinch of salt
  4. Enjoy the best lunch ever!
